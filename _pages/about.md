I am a theoretical physicist working on a broad range of topics in quantum matter and quantum information: topological band structures, intrinsic topological orders and fractionalizations, exotic quantum phase transitions, Moire materials, quantum information and dynamical systems. 

See my [google scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=fyVphr4AAAAJ&hl=en) for publications. 

* 2011-2015: California Institute of Technology 
(advisor: [John Preskill](http://www.its.caltech.edu/~preskill))

* 2015-2020: Harvard University 
(advisor: [Ashvin Vishwanath](https://www.physics.harvard.edu/people/facpages/vishwanath))

* 2020-2023: Moore Fellow at <a href='https://www.kitp.ucsb.edu/'>Kavli Institute of Theoretical Physics</a>
